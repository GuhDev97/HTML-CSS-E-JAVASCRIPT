Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for ony meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

    neat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pépper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

    Fold and serve:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pépper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
